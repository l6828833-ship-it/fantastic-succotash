== Description ==

**Chatrico adds an AI chat bot and live chat to your WordPress site.** It answers your customers automatically 24/7, captures leads, and hands off to a real human on your team when needed — all from one widget.

This plugin connects your WordPress site to your Chatrico account. Log in once, pick your AI agent, and the chat widget goes live. You can also manage everything right inside WordPress.

**What you can do from WordPress:**

* **AI chat bot** on your site — answers from your own content, 24/7.
* **Live chat & human handoff** — take over a conversation any time.
* **Log in** with your Chatrico account, right in wp-admin.
* **Usage board** — see AI conversations, contacts, agents and tickets against your plan.
* **Agents** — see your AI agents and pick which one runs on this site.
* **Inbox** — read live conversations without leaving WordPress.
* **Plans** — compare plans and upgrade in a click.

Tickets, contacts, analytics and billing open in your full Chatrico dashboard.

Appearance (color, position, size, theme) is managed in your Agent Settings on chatrico.com and applies automatically — you never re-copy code.

Chatrico is a hosted service (Software as a Service). You need a free account at [chatrico.com](https://chatrico.com). A free plan is available with no credit card.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `chatrico` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`, or install the ZIP via Plugins → Add New → Upload.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Go to **Chatrico → Connect** and log in with your Chatrico email and password.
4. Pick which AI agent shows on your site and save. The chat widget goes live instantly.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a Chatrico account? =
Yes. Create one free at https://chatrico.com — no credit card required.

= Is this a live chat or an AI chat bot? =
Both. The AI chat bot answers automatically, and you can take over with live chat (human handoff) whenever you want.

= Will it slow down my site? =
No. The widget loads asynchronously from Chatrico, so it won’t block your pages.

= How do I change the widget color or position? =
In your Chatrico Agent Settings. Changes apply automatically; you don’t edit this plugin.

= Where do I reply to conversations? =
Open the Inbox from the plugin’s Inbox page (the “Open Inbox in Chatrico” button) to chat in real time and hand off between AI and your team.
